Events & Projects

We believe that Slovak culture should be experienced, not just learned. That’s why we organise a variety of events for everyone—families, children, adults, and individuals who simply want to connect with Slovak heritage, make new friends, and enjoy great company.

Upcoming Events

Easter Workshop (6th April)

Step into the world of Slovak Easter traditions at our Easter Workshop, where culture, creativity, and community come together for a fun and welcoming afternoon.

Kids Disco 2025 (12th July)

A high-energy event to celebrate the end of the school year with music, dancing, and lots of fun! This special edition will include a "Vysvedčenie" ceremony where children will receive their end-of-year certificates.

Past Events

Christmas Workshop

A festive gathering with handmade decorations, Slovak carols, and traditional Christmas treats.
